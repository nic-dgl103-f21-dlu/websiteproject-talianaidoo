ABOUT US

Dave's Bakery is a locally owned and operated wholesale bakery and retail cafe. We specialise in bread making and pastries that made with organic and locally sources ingredients. All breads and pastries are made by hand, from scratch using old techniques and pure ingredients.

OUR TEAM

Our team is committed to giving you the best bread and pastries and experience. We are a team of fun, diverse people who have a shared passion for baking and putting smiles on our customers faces!
